gcharang 08-Jan-20 04:00 PM
@CoinKing88 when you move DEX out of ledger, the privatekey of Komodo (as they are the same) will also be exposed to a computer. If that is ok, here are the steps: To store: 1) Just send to a KMD address from your Ledger To move them: 1) Open this webpage in an offline computer: https://iancoleman.io/bip39/ 2) Input your ledger seed words in the field "BIP39 Mnemonic" 3) Select KMD in the field named "Coin" 4) In "Derivation path", select the tab "BIP44" 5) you should see the addresses and privatekeys of your ledger addresses 6) copy the privatekey of the address with your DEX coin and use it to login to Verus Agama and you can move them
