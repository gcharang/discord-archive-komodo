How it works... is something like this, (this is my understanding anyway) PoS ... it calculates if it can win, if it cant then it does not try... as time passes, the calculation becomes easier, until a utxo can stake and tries. PoW ... is PoW BUT ... if the PoS % is too low the PoW difficulty increases exponentially really really fast until the PoW cannot find a block. (edited)
This means without enough hash rate, the PoS can just over take the PoW because it gets an easier difficulty after 384s passes since the last block, PoS will always find a block, has upsides nad down sides, upside is chain can never stall, and hash attacks dont happen. (edited)
Down side is blocks can be a little inconsitant ... although not as bad as BTC is.
I am really looking foward to the main chain, to see what happens with a high hash rate and a long enough time period, if the inconsitent blocks stop happening.
