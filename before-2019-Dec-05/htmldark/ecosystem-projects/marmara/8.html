dimxy 28-Jun-19 10:04 AM
LCL values are empty because they are null before the test and should be clear after the settlement (the table shows the status after the settlement). So the results are correct (as I understood the algorithm ) Total credit amount was 10 coins. After the settlement those 10 coins eventually were sent to pk3 normal. For pk1 its activated account decreased by 10 coins and normal account increased by 5 coins. These 5 coins were sent to its normal when the look-in-loop amount was redistributed between pk1 and pk2 at the moment when the baton was sent to pk3. For pk2 its activated amount is decreased by 5 coins (which he donated to the loop). The settlement now is automatic and is started by any miner. (And it creates a problem: the miner who started the settlement cannot send the baton to the issuer as required by the document. The baton is on the holder's pubkey and the miner cannot spend it)
