@yorklab the key to answering the question is you knowing what you want long-term and if you can afford scale.

If it takes 2 months to reach positive ROI on deviceX and you can get 12 of them, then you can get 12 more in 2 months. If you only get 1 at a time, then your growth rate will be exponentially slower.
My personal philosophy, as cliche as it is, is "go big or go home".
