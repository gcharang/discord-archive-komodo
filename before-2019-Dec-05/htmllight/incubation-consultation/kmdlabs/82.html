Alright 11-Nov-18 08:18 PM
Yea we really need to concise explanation of what it is. To simplify(a lot) it's a platform for testing smart contracts. A coin dev wouldn't want their first iteration of their smart contract in their coin immediately as it might require many hard forks to get right. With STAKED, dev can launch a chain with their own contract, if there is a problem, they simply kill the chain, update the contract and start another
STAKED is also the only way to have opt-in crypto condition contracts as opposed to compulsory consensus rules
if a normal asset chain wants to add/update a contract, all nodes need to update (edited)
with STAKED, not the case
If you want to use a contract on STAKED, you just sync the chain it's on and transfer coins to it
