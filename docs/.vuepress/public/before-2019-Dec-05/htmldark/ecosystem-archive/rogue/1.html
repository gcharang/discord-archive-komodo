Hello Everyone! I would really like to sort out some branding for The Rogue project. I feel like we should really lock in a well chosen title for the project. I ended up putting "Rogue Chain" on twitter to get some accounts started, but i'm not happy with it. I have a list put together for possible names to choose from and am open to more. I think selecting one banner to fly under is one of the most important things we can all decide on as it brings brand recognition, sets us apart from the other projects and shows strength as a community. I would like pretty much anyone's opinion so please feel free to tell what you like/do not like and WHY. Outside of my personal ideas I have seen Roguelike and Roguelander used alongside my current "Rogue Chain" . SO here is the list along with some comments from me. 1 Roguelander - what JL777c has named the multpilayer game play(aka highlander can only be one.) 2 Roguelike - Is a genre and technically... this game we have is ACTUALLY Rogue ..... so Roguelike as a title doesn't sit well for me. 3 RogueCC - This grew on me over time, it showcases Crypto Conditions as the base of technology, it is short, simple and clear. It even has a nice ring to it and has this subconscious implication of being fast (500CC etc) 4 Rogue by Komodo 5 Komodo Rogue 6 KMD Rogue 7 Roguechain 8 Rogue Chain (I'm personally burnt on the chain crypto names) 9 Rogue Reinvented 10(A) Rogue Legacy - I really dig this one. 11 (B)Chain Rogue 12(C) Crypto Rogue 13(D) Rogue+ 14(E) Rogue++ Any other ideas?
