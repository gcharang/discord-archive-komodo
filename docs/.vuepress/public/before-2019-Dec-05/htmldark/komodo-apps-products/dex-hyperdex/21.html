lukechilds 17-Sep-18 09:07 AM
We encrypt your seed and store it on your machine, similar to how electrum does
when you login and enter your password, your seed is decrypted
so if someone gains access to your machine or your backups, your funds are safe, assuming you have a sufficiently strong password
hardware wallet support isn't possible for us to add at the GUI level, but if it's first supported upstream in marketmaker it's something we could implement (edited)
