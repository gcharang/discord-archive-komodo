fundamentally an atomic swap is a trustless tx on two blockchains, as opposed to a CEX that is a DB entry for funds that have been deposited that has to be withdrawn. Most of the CEX now require burdensome privacy invasion to withdraw your coins. if that is ok, then you can pretend to be trading the coins in 1 to 2 seconds and trust that you will be able to withdraw your coins.
From a maker point of view, trades takes a lot longer than a few seconds, even on CEX as they have to wait for someone to take their offer.
maybe having the trades taking more than a few seconds will lead to less panic based trades and more thoughtful trades? it would be fantastic if we can get the speed with all the other benefits, but without removing the blockchain throughput bottleneck and then the data storage/sync bottleneck, it seems we are stuck with the relatively slow atomic swaps.
