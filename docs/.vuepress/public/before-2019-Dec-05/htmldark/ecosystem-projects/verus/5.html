[dscrdvrsc] <VirtualSoundNW> The difficulty is a moving average that adjusts to get to a 1 block per minute rate. If the hash rate increases the block rate goes up, then over several samples the higher rate will cause the difficulty to go up, slowing the rate back down towards 1/minute. Decreases in network hash slow the rate, triggering a decrease in difficulty over multiple samples to speed it back up towards 1 per minute.
