[23-Nov-18 12:54 PM] Alright#0419
So dev has a new contract they need tested
they create a chain
burn the premine about of this chain on main LABS chain
fund the notaries on their new chain with premine amount(notaries can choose what they want to be paid), 
testers transfer some amount of LABS to the new chain to use in testing
Tester finds a bug. 
Tester posts a bounty claim via oracles on main chain. 
Notaries discuss whether this claim is fair. If it is, they sign off on it. New coins are created and given to tester. The amount of coins that can be created in this process is limited on a weekly basis.
