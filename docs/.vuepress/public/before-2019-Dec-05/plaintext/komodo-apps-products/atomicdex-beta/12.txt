[17-Jul-19 06:12 AM] artem.pikulin#0794 (pinned)
@PTYX Hi, the process of setting up a LP node (maker) is as follows::
1. Arrange a server/vps.
2. Build/download MM2 (atomicDEX-API) and configure following walkthrough guideline: https://developers.atomicdex.io/basic-docs/atomicdex/atomicdex-tutorials/atomicdex-walkthrough.html#initiate-marketmaker-2-0
3. Activate the coins you'd like to sell and receive, fill the balance with desired amount.
4. Call `setprice` for selected pairs: https://developers.atomicdex.io/basic-docs/atomicdex/atomicdex-api.html#setprice
5. If you have any questions please feel free to ask at #dev-marketmaker
