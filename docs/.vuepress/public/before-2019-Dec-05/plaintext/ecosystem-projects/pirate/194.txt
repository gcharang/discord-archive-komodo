Soaks the desired amount onto all online and idle users (optionally, within specified role), e.g.
`!arrrtip soak 5.00`, `!arrrtip soak 5.00 @Supporters`
`!arrrtip cannon <amount> [<@role>]`
Tips 2-5 random lucky online user with the amount (optionally, within specified role), e.g.
`!arrrtip cannon 5.00`, `!arrrtip cannon 5.00 @Supporters`
