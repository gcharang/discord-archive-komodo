There are also 'Zs' addresses or Z addresses, these are private addresses where balances do not show up on chains. They also make Z transactions where the amount and addresses aren't visible either. What I mean by this is Komodo uses multi-chain infrastructure, it's how they scale, so Z transactions are going onto a new parallel chain along with new Crypto Conditions smart contracts. This swapping between parallel chains will be seamless and using Komodos MoMoM tech to ensure fungible coins and keep supply consistent.
