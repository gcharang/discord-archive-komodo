`the main issue with iguana is TIME_WAIT on local host` ... yes, and it is only connections from iguana to the coins RPC port... wonder if iguana could manage this better, to reduce the number of TIME_WAIT connections... normally it is not a big problem, even with 10s of thousands of such connections, but the higher the number, the bigger the chance that you will have trouble starting up the wallets, because the rpcport they try to listen to is already in use by such a TIME_WAIT connection... but if you manage to start your wallets, it doesn't matter any more
