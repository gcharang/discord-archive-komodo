ca333 28-Jul-19 09:16 AM
@kartha87 we actually updated it the other way around. So first we did allow just raw hex (which represents a ETH address) without further validation but noticed that this could cause issues (users sending to a wrong address) - that is why we implemented https://eips.ethereum.org/EIPS/eip-55 - to prevent this risk. (edited)
Mixed-case checksum address encoding
Ethereum Improvement Proposals (EIPs) describe standards for the Ethereum platform, including core protocol specifications, client APIs, and contract standards.
to answer your question: best practise is to use case-sensitive format to prevent the issues and enjoy the benefits described in eip55
