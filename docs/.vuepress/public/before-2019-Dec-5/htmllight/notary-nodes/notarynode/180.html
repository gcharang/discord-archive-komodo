Decker 30-Apr-19 08:00 AM
Awesome! Main purpose of this puzzle was to force people learn something new. So, seems idea was good, i'm glad! Btw, there is an additional extra hint exists on blockchain all of these time. If you will see txes on bTMf2oY21U63oZD6Yfk1igyp4MjcaEfp8m you will see, that it already has spent tx b001d94442f3ce5e38ae845ceaee9a9f5de95370f4e3a5314aa7e8fc714eeb71 from this address. So, users initially have 2 possible variants of solution: 1. Look on the picture, trying to hash vote4decker secret and construct a reedem script. 2. Or just look on txes, and copy complete redeem script from tx b001d94442f3ce5e38ae845ceaee9a9f5de95370f4e3a5314aa7e8fc714eeb71 without know a secret and without know how to construct it. Like "monkey copy style" )
--- Solution: https://komodod.com/t/945b6d970204bf414102a4dfadec8615c09ab48c20d835e77f488bcbd78e4599 Look at the winner tx and it's scriptSig field:
0b766f7465346465636b6572aa201b549cfc12bd56569b5d1b73fa5cdda12ca5f681c648328b40445db4f41e15f587
As it P2SH, scriptSig field contains here redeem script:
766f7465346465636b6572 OP_HASH256 1b549cfc12bd56569b5d1b73fa5cdda12ca5f681c648328b40445db4f41e15f5 OP_EQUAL
As you see 766f7465346465636b6572 it's a vote4decker in ASCII, that gives you 1b549cfc12bd56569b5d1b73fa5cdda12ca5f681c648328b40445db4f41e15f5 after OP_SHA256. So, two steps for solve needed: 1. Found secret vote4decker 2. Construct above redeem script. 3. Make a tx with passing this redeem script to claim the reward. (edited)
