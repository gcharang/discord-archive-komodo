One of the tricks is when a user stakes the HEX are burnt, reducing circulating supply numbers on CMC, if all goes to plan 90% of supply is locked up in stakes, so HEX will appear on CMC much lower down the rankings, making it look cheap. Smoke and mirrors, but if the staked amounts stay consistent the circulating supply will too.
